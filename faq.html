Frequently Asked Questions

What file types are supported?

This converter supports JPG and JPEG formats. It does not accept PNG, GIF, or other formats in the current version — you can open those in an image editor and save as JPG before converting.

Is my uploaded image or data stored on the server?

No. All conversions happen securely in your browser using client-side scripts. Your files are not uploaded or stored on any external server, ensuring your privacy and security.

Do I need to sign up or create an account?

No account or sign-up is required. All tools on dhirajgurung.com.np are free to use without registration or login.

Can I use these tools on mobile devices?

Yes! All tools are designed to be mobile-friendly and work seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Simply open the site in your mobile browser to start using any tool.

Are there any usage limits?

No hard limits are imposed for normal personal use. However, automated or bulk use of the tools may be restricted to maintain server performance and fair access for everyone.

How accurate are the results?

Each tool is tested and optimized to deliver accurate results. However, since browser environments may vary slightly, we recommend double-checking critical outputs before using them in professional settings.

Who created these tools?

All tools are developed by Dhiraj Gurung, a Nepal-based web developer focused on building free, privacy-friendly, and efficient online utilities for everyday use.

How can I give feedback or suggest new tools?

You can share feedback, bug reports, or feature requests through the Contact page. Your suggestions help improve and expand the collection of free tools.

Is there a cost to use these tools?

No. All tools available on dhirajgurung.com.np are completely free to use — no subscriptions, hidden charges, or downloads required.

How often are the tools updated?

Tools are regularly updated for performance improvements, bug fixes, and new features. Check back often or follow Dhiraj Gurung on social platforms for announcements about new releases.
